Who are we?

“LTP” Long-Term Planing Public Union is a national NGO working towards building better civil society. The organization is located in Budapest region in Hungary. The process to establish “LTP” began on 2018. The main purpose of “LTP ” is to to provide the opportunity for young people and individuals for reaching their full potential that will support them in transforming their own lives. To create new creative and innovative educational programs for and with young people to develop their competencies and skills.

To support sustainable development of society and economical growth. “Long-Term Planing” Youth Public Union strives to increase youth participation in activities, support disabled people in personal development and acquiring necessary job search skills, develop intellectual abilities and professional skills of youth, involve youth in volunteer work, form civil society through the youth movement, ensure youth participation in environmental actions and tourism, and to develop mutual relationships between Hungarian and world youth. In pursuit of the above-mentioned objectives, Union regulary provides local, national and international seminars, conventions, meetings, training courses, consultative services, and work camps.
